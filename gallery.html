Gallery

Take a look at some of our other products. Be sure to check back often as a large amount of our stock is seasonal and changes.

Gourmet Foods

The staple of the gift basket, our gourmet foods selection provides unique and flavorful ideas for your next gift. Enjoy our vast selection of jams, jellies, spreads and sauces!

Bath and Oils

Soak away your troubles with our bath oils and soaps collection. There's nothing better than sitting in your tub with a glass of wine and scent of our bath products to bring you to nirvana.

Seasonal

As the seasons change, so too does our stock. From warm sweaters and mittens in the fall and winter to hats and parisols for the spring and summer, you will always be able to find something for the season.

One of a Kind Items

Somethings just don't fit a category but are too unique and interesting to pass up. Take a look at our one of a kind items. Perfect gifts for the person who's hard to shop for.

Hand-made Jewelry

For a taste of luxury and class, take a look at our hand-crafted jewelry. Each piece is unique and lovingly crafted. Budget meets beauty in these fantastically beautiful necklaces, bracelets and broaches.

Kid's Corner

Don't be afraid to bring your kids to our colorful kid's corner. Our selection of puzzles, dolls, craft sets and plushies make great gifts for kids.

Fudge and Candy

Stop by for a free piece of fudge! All of our fudge is made locally. Our candy selection rivals our gourmet foods section with specialties like maple candies, chocolate covered coffee beans and jelly beans!
