About Us

Le Panier Champêtre is owned and operated by Annette Blanco and Patti Larose-Mason. We are passionate about bringing the joy of gift giving to our clients. Le Panier Champêtre has been creating customized gift baskets for corporate and personal occasions since 2004. Desgined with classic style, each gift basket is put together carefully with attention to every detail. Product selection and presentation are a main focus for us in creating a gift basket that is just right for you.
